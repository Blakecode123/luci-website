Lucinda’s journey

Lucinda is a former engineer who, like many people in demanding careers, once lived in a constant cycle of stress and overthinking. Being naturally analytical, she didn’t turn to yoga, meditation, breathwork and sound out of blind belief. The method needed to have scientifically backed mechanisms and produce effects she could genuinely feel. Through dedicated practice, these methods naturally healed her anxiety for good, while also opening the door to powerful experiences of consciousness she hadn’t known were possible. What began as a personal transformation quickly became something she felt compelled to share. Since 2020, Lucinda has been guiding others through these practices. She has helped people quiet busy minds, reset their nervous systems, and experience the same deeply calming and often profound states that first changed her own life.
